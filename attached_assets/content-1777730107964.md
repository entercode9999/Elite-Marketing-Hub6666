02.How do you approach web designing?

We follow these key stages:

**Discover**

The first step to approaching a web design project is to study the project deeply. It includes:

- Understanding your niche
- Identifying your business’s strengths and weaknesses
- Assessing how credible your business appears to the users
- Crafting a detailed buyer persona to define your target audience
- Knowing your business’s short and long-term goals
- Gathering a list of your products and services
- Finding a USP (Unique Selling Proposition)

**Plan**

Once we have a thorough knowledge of your business, we start with the planning phase. Here is how we proceed:

- Select the ideal technology and platform to meet your needs
- Prepare a perfect layout plan or architecture for your website
- Think of the best mega menu structure
- Decide on the content style (formal, informal, or casual) required
- Deciphering Branding Details – Colours, Fonts, Logo
- Determining the total number of web pages required
- Finding a suitable content management system
- Making a hyperlinking plan

**Design**

The next step that follows after the planning phase is – DESIGNING. We prepare a design for the website. How do we do it?

- Prepare a layout for different sections of the homepage
- Create a story-like flow in different sections of the page
- Consider website elements from an SEO perspective
- Prepare a graphics requirement sheet
- Try to deliver an out-of-the-box design
- Work on typography and colour combinations
- Allocate space for seamless integration of functionality

**Develop**

The development phase infuses life into the designed page. Our expert team of web developers incorporates advanced functionality into the page. What do they do?

- Direct Call-To-Action to Lead Capturing Pages
- Ease the site navigation
- Boost site security
- Work to reduce site load speed
- Enhance the capacity of a website to handle large traffic
- Manage payment gateways
- Instill magical elements in the website

**Launch**

The launch is the most satisfying stage of the web design process. It is the stage which finally lets your efforts bear fruit. What happens in the Launch phase?

- Every element is double-checked for any errors
- The QA team is on a mission to find the bugs and propose solutions
- Manual & Automated Testing of UX and UI
- Checking whether the website aligns with the client’s requirements
- Checking if the website is built considering the targeted audience
- Finding any room for improvement
- Inspecting if there is any lack of security incorporation

**Promote**

Let’s move on to the last stage – PROMOTION. As soon as search engines consume a site, digital marketers finish their work. What do they do?

- Get your site indexed and ranked on the 1st page of SERP
- Create a comprehensive digital marketing strategy
- Enhance your brand presence on all online platforms
- Boost the credibility and authority of your website
- Activate email marketing, SMO, PPC, and SEO campaigns
- Implement remarketing campaigns to bring users back to the website
- Work on a client-retention campaign
