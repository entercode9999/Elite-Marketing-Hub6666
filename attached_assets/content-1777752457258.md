## FAQs

01.Are web design and UI/UX the same?

UX and UI form a major part of web design.

02.How much does web design cost?

For a basic site, you can expect to pay around $1000. But to develop a custom website, you might have to pay between $5,000 and $20,000. For a more detailed breakdown, consult our website design expert. We can help you understand your needs and select the right services.

03.How much time does it take to make an e-commerce website?

The time required to create an [e-commerce](https://www.kinexmedia.com/ecommerce/) website varies based on the complexity of the design, the features needed, and the extent of customization and development.

04.How much time does it take to redesign a website?

Redesign timelines depend on the scope of the project. Factors include:

- Extent of the redesign (minor tweaks vs. complete overhaul).
- Complexity of the website.
